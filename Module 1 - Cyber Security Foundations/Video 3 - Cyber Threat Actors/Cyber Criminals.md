

**Motivation: Profit**

- **Definition**: These are individuals or professional groups, sometimes operating as **Cybercrime Syndicates**, who conduct attacks primarily for financial gain.
    
- **Behavior**: They often use automated tools or **CaaS (Cybercrime-as-a-Service)** kits to cast a wide net, looking for the **"Path of Least Resistance"** among SMEs.
    
- **Common Attacks**: They are the primary users of **Ransomware**, **Business Email Compromise (BEC)**, and data theft for sale on the dark web.
