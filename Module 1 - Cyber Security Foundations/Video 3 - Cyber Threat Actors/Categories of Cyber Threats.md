
|Threat Actor|Skills|Primary Objectives|Typical Targets|Common Examples & Roles|
|---|---|---|---|---|
|**Thrill Seekers**|**Low**|Fun / Satisfaction|Individuals, SMEs, or Corporations|Phishing, Malware; often act as entry-level **Hackers**.|
|**Hacktivists**|**Low to Medium**|Revenge / Monetary Gain / Ideological|Corporations or Individuals|Destruction and Theft; may use **Hosted System Providers** for leaks.|
|**Insiders**|**Low to Medium**|Defamation / Notoriety / Discontent|Corporations, Individuals, or Governments|DDoS, Anonymous, WikiLeaks, LulzSec; acts as a direct **Distributor** of stolen data.|
|**Terrorists**|**Low to Medium**|Fundraising / Propaganda / Recruitment|All entities|Al-Qaeda sites, ISIS recruitment; utilize **Fraudsters** for social engineering.|
|**Organized Crime**|**High**|Financial Gain / Profit|Retailers, Financial Services, Individuals|Carding, ACH PII; run by **Organizational Leaders** with **Money Mules** and **Tellers**.|
|**State Sponsored**|**Very High**|Economic Advantage / Geopolitical|Intellectual Property, R&D, Weapons, Legal|APTs, ICS SCADA, Chinese Hackers; utilize top-tier **Programmers** and **Tech Experts**.|

### Key Takeaways

- **Skill vs. Impact:** As the skill level moves from **Low** to **Very High**, the targets shift from random individuals/SMEs to critical national infrastructure and high-value intellectual property.
    
- **The "Money Trail":** Financial Gain (Organized Crime) and Economic Advantage (State Sponsored) represent the most sophisticated threats because they have the budget to hire all **10 Underground Roles**.
    
- **The SME Danger Zone:** Most SMEs fall into the "Low to Medium" skill target range, but they are increasingly targeted by **Organized Crime** looking for high-volume, low-effort financial gains.
