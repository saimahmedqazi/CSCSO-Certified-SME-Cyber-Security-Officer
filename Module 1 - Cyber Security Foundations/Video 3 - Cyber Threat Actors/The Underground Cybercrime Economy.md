### 1. Organizational Leaders

- **Context:** These are the "Project Managers" of the criminal world.
    
- **Connection:** Usually high-level Cyber Criminals motivated by Profit.
    
- **Function:** Often "people persons" without deep technical skills; they assemble the team and choose the target.
    

### 2. Programmers

- **Context:** The R&D department of the syndicate.
    
- **Connection:** They provide the tools for Nation States (espionage) or Cyber Criminals (ransomware).
    
- **Function:** They develop the exploits and malware used to commit the actual crimes.
    

### 3. Distributors

- **Context:** The brokers or "Logistics" layer.
    
- **Connection:** They facilitate the trade of data stolen by Insiders or Thrill Seekers.
    
- **Function:** They trade and sell stolen data and act as escrows for goods/services provided by other specialists.
    

### 4. Tech Experts

- **Context:** The back-end IT support.
    
- **Connection:** They ensure the Cyber Criminal's infrastructure is "always on".
    
- **Function:** Maintain the infrastructure, including servers, encryption technologies, and databases.
    

### 5. Hackers

- **Context:** The "Infiltration" specialists.
    
- **Connection:** Their primary motivation is often Satisfaction (Thrill Seekers) or Geopolitical (Nation States).
    
- **Function:** They search for and exploit applications, systems, and network vulnerabilities to gain access.
    

### 6. Fraudsters

- **Context:** The social engineering department.
    
- **Connection:** They exploit Inadequate Training in SMEs to bypass Insufficient Cyber Defenses.
    
- **Function:** Create and deploy various social engineering schemes, such as phishing and spam.
    

### 7. Hosted System Providers

- **Context:** The "Landlords" of the dark web.
    
- **Connection:** Used by Hacktivists or Terrorist Groups to host propaganda or stolen leaks safely.
    
- **Function:** Offer "bulletproof hosting" of illicit content, servers, and sites that ignore takedown requests.
    

### 8. Cashiers

- **Context:** The first layer of financial laundering.
    
- **Connection:** Purely motivated by Profit; they facilitate the payoff for Cyber Criminals.
    
- **Function:** Control drop accounts and provide names/accounts to other criminals for a fee.
    

### 9. Money Mules

- **Context:** The "Foot Soldiers" of the money trail.
    
- **Connection:** Often "unwitting" agents recruited through fake job ads.
    
- **Function:** Complete electronic transfers between bank accounts to move illicit funds.
    

### 10. Tellers

- **Context:** The "Bankers" for the syndicate.
    
- **Connection:** The final stage of the Profit motivation.
    
- **Function:** Charged with transferring and laundering illicitly gained proceeds through digital/crypto currency services.
