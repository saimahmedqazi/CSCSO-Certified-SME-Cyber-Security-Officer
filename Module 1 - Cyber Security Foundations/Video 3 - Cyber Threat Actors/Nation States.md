
**Motivation: Geopolitical**

- **Definition**: These are state-sponsored groups or **Advanced Persistent Threats (APTs)** funded by national governments.
    
- **Behavior**: They are highly patient and well-resourced. Instead of "smash and grab" tactics, they often hide in a network for months or years to conduct **Espionage** (stealing secrets) or map out critical infrastructure.
    
- **SME Link**: They often target SMEs as a **[[Supply Chain Gateway]]** to reach the larger government or defense targets they are actually interested in.
