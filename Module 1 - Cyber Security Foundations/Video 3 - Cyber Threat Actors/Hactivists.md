**Motivation: Ideological**

- **Definition**: Hacktivists are individuals or groups who use hacking to promote a political agenda, social change, or a specific ideology.
    
- **Behavior**: Their goal is usually to gain attention for a cause or to embarrass an organization they disagree with. They often engage in **Website Defacement** (changing the look of a site) or **DDoS attacks** (flooding a site with traffic to take it offline).
    
- **SME Link**: While they often target large corporations or government agencies, an SME might be targeted if it is seen as supporting a controversial cause or if it's a partner of a larger organization the group is protesting.
